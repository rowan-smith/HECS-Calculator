HECS debt can be hard, but it shouldn't be.
The following are facts about HECS debt:

  • HECS Debt is indexed annually on June 1
  • 'Indexation' is the same as interest on a loan, just different terminology
  • Only HECS debt you have had for more than 1 year has indexation applied to it
  • You only make automatic HECS repayments when your annual income is above a certain amount
  • In majority of cases, making a $25-$50 weekly voluntary contribution will save you years and thousands of dollars

This site has a calculator that allows you to compare the time and money saved by making a theoretical weekly payment
Ensure you try a few different numbers to get an idea of what weekly payment amount best suits you
